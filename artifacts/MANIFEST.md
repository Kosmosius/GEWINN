# Artifact manifest

`artifacts/raw/` holds large, reproducible raw outputs that should remain local
and Git-ignored. `artifacts/tmp/` is disposable local working state. Record a
path, size, SHA-256, provenance, and regeneration command here before relying
on any raw artifact.

## Imported raw artifacts

None. The imported project had no large reproducible raw simulation tables or
archives; its source binaries and historical images are preserved in `archive/`
and tracked as historical evidence.
